About


How does this place make you feel?


“Happy Here” is a mobile app for spatial emotional storytelling.


What places make you happy? The pizzeria where your grandmother brought you to have your first slice? The bar where you met your boyfriend and made out all night on the saggy couch? The quietest patch of grass in Prospect Park where you go to daydream? Here, you can share the story behind what connects you to this happy place.


Our long-term goal is to encourage people to build narratives of their communities by mapping the positive experiences people have had and adding new layers of history. This creates a collective memory for everyone to see and share.
